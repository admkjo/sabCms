             Editing Pages


     1. Click the corresponding  "select" button under the 'options column' of the page you want to edit.

             


           2.The details of the content you want to edit will populate in the forms above where you can make your changes.

                   

           

                3.  You can delete a resource for a content or add a resource for a content whiles editing the page.

                      When done with the changes, click on the save edited content under the form for your

              changes to take effect.


                 4.The rest form button when clicked will clear all details populated in the form.
