             Deleting Pages


     1. Click the corresponding  "select" button under the 'options column' of the page you want to delete.

             


           2.The details of the content you want to delete will populate in the forms above .

                   

           

                3.  Click on the "Delete Content"  under the form to delete the content


                 4.The rest form button when clicked will clear all details populated in the form.
